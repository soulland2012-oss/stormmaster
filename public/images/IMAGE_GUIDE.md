# Stormmaster Archive — Image Guide

## Folder structure

public/images/
  heroes/      ← full-screen cinematic headers (1920×1080+)
  gallery/     ← article gallery images
  portraits/   ← character portrait frames
  world/       ← world / map visuals

---

## How to replace a placeholder

In the code, each image slot is already named.
Drop the file into the correct folder with the exact filename below
and the site will automatically use it.

## HEROES — full-screen header images

### heroes/kaslana-hero.jpg
Used on: House Kaslana article header
Dimensions: 1920×1080 minimum (wider is better, 2560×1440 ideal)
Orientation: Landscape / panoramic

Concept:
  A grand cathedral-fortress carved into a white mountain cliff at dawn.
  Golden light pours from above (sun, possibly through stained glass).
  In the foreground or center — a lone knight in white-gold plate armor
  stands at the edge of a high balcony or gate, looking outward.
  The architecture is massive, gothic, with sharp vertical spires.
  Aura particles (golden specks of light) float upward.

Mood: Holy, epic, cinematic, dawn light
Colors: White, ivory, gold, warm amber, pale sky blue
Avoid: Red, green, modern elements, tech
AI prompt keywords: "cathedral fortress mountain dawn, white gold knight silhouette,
  light rays, volumetric light, gothic spires, epic fantasy, cinematic"

## Style reference (show this to AI art tools)

Overall art direction:
  - Painterly, not photorealistic
  - Cinematic lighting — single strong light source
  - High contrast between light and shadow
  - Elden Ring / Dark Souls atmospheric density
  - League of Legends / Genshin Impact character clarity
  - Warhammer 40K architecture scale
  - Apple-level visual polish and composition

What to AVOID:
  - Cartoon / anime style (unless it matches your vision)
  - Flat lighting / evenly lit scenes
  - Modern/contemporary elements
  - Photographic realism without painterly treatment
  - Cluttered compositions — rule of thirds, clean focal points
